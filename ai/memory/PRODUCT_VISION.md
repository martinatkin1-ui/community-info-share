# Product Vision

## Problem Statement
People and professionals often struggle to find the right local support, understand eligibility, and complete referral actions efficiently. Organisations also struggle to present services and events consistently.

## Value Proposition
A platform that makes community support easier to publish, easier to find, and easier to refer into.

## Primary Outcomes
- better access to relevant services and events
- lower admin burden for professionals and organisations
- stronger referral completion and follow-through
- clearer evidence of value and impact

## Value by Persona
### Organisations
- publish and maintain services/events easily
- increase visibility and uptake

### Providers / Referrers
- find suitable support quickly
- refer with less friction
- trust the quality of information

### Clients / Citizens
- discover support more easily
- self-refer with confidence
- understand next steps clearly

### Administrators
- govern quality, access, and reliability
- evidence activity and value

## Product Principles
- clarity over complexity
- trust through transparency
- accessibility by default
- low-friction critical flows
- outcome-oriented design

## Adoption and Trust Principles
- publishing must be simple and reliable
- search results must feel relevant
- referrals must be trackable
- permissions and data handling must feel safe
